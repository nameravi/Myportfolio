Brand Comfort

Client Unilever , UK

Objective

Create a package design or still advert for Comfort's new liquid Fabric Conditioner that communicates 'freshness' and 'naturalness' in a way that's irresistible and distinct from existing fabric conditioners.

Idea description

The idea is to blend the clothes and nature seamlessly. This is shown by a series ofimages which could be used to different variants of the products. We show three images of nature - Snowclad Mountain, Lush Green landscape, Sunny Dessert. While on first look it looks like an image of nature, but on closer, it becomes visible that the snow-clad mountain is not a mountain but a white cloth folded in the shape of a mountain. Similarly, in the green landscape, the green grass is actually a green towel, and the dessert sand dunes are nothing but a brown jacket.

The visual shows how the clothes blend into nature just like what our product offers. The Tagline, “Blending the freshness of nature' reiterates the visual - blending the clothes with the nature freshness. The word blend also has another connotation. The comfort conditioner is BLENDED (mixed) with the washing powder to get the freshness of nature. The circle illustrated around the copy is an signifies the blending process of mixing of the conditioner with the clothes in the washing machine or bucket, incidentally both has circle circumference.

Brand AXA

Objective

To craft a compelling advertising campaign that highlights how the insurance company values and rewards customer loyalty in innovative and meaningful ways, strengthening long-term relationships and trust.

Idea description

In today's world, digital security & protection is very important as lot of classified information are stored in the personal devices like mobile laptops desktops etc., Now, AXA, as a part of its reward program, offers its customers digital protection by providing an anti-virus software subscription valid for one year for one of their digital devices. The subscription gets renewed every year depending on the term of the insurance contract. So, if the contract is for 5 years, then the subscription is valid for five years; but would be renewed every year. And for customer who have more than 6 contracts, the annual subscription would be for multiple devices. This reward program essentially underlines the fact that AXA as an insurance company ensure the protection of its customer digital part as well.

how your fidelity reward/ feature(s) could improve clients' satisfaction?

Digital devices are an integral part of our life. everyone is on it for lot many purposes like social media, banking etc. where there is a high risk of information theft. In such a scenario, digital security is more than important now. And most people, though are aware of the impending risk, don't usually take concrete measure to make it secure. So, this reward defending an individual and his entire family against virus, malware, random ware etc., would be a perfect compliment as it gives them peace of mind in both virtual & real life.

How it works?

AXA has to reach out to either one of the major players in antivirus software like Kaspersky, Norton, ESET or McAfee or any other credible antivirus software company. An agreement has to be made where AXA brings in a certain amount of customer from its domain while the software company would provide the annual subscription at a nominal value which would benefit both the companies, in terms of increasing the customer base as well the revenue part.

Brand Spadel

Client SV Spadel NV, Belgium

Idea description

The idea is to provide the consumer a single bottle which could be refilled anywhere according to their convenience based on subscription. So, the consumer buys a bottled natural water to drink, once he consumes the waters, proceeds to scans the code embedded sticker at the bottom of the bottle with his mobile phone, downloads the app to registers and subscribe to a certain number of liters which needs to be consumed within a certain timeframe. (For example: 50 liters for one month time). And next time when he feels like drinking water, he could just go to the nearest Refiller tap to fill his empty old bottle. The Refiller tap is installed in every nook and corner of the city and this tap spot could be located with the help of the downloaded App. The consumer could refill how much ever liters he/she has subscribed for. First time, the consumer buys the bottle filled with natural water, once he drinks the water, he can fold the bottle flat vertically and keep it in the bag. The collapsible / accordion fold design of the bottle makes this possible. And next time when he wants to drink the water, he opens the Refill App, locates the nearest Refilling tap, pulls out the folded bottle from his bag, takes it close by the machine, the machine has a sensor which detects the bottle and the slot opens automatically. There is a hollow space with a tap on the top and a magnetic plate at the bottom. The bottle needs to be kept in a folded format only. The code at the bottom of the bottle sits perfectly on the magnetic plate and helps the machine to read the code and its corresponding details of the consumer, his subscription plan etc., and based on this, the machine automatically pulls up the bottle and fills water based on the size of the bottle. Here it needs to be noted, the consumer doesn't have the choice to decide on the quantity, for example, he cannot fill half. Here the filling is automated, once the bottle goes in, the machine pulls out and read the size and fills in accordingly. The filling hardly takes a minute to fill and the message appears on the black door. After few seconds, the door opens, the bottle is filled with fresh water. The consumer could do this repeatedly until the subscription exhausts. This subscription cost would be very less when comparing buying individual bottle every now and then.

what is it, how does it work, in home, out of home, what does the service look like, how does the water/packaging go from the provider to the end user (and back), This idea is primarily for outdoors, for people who are on the go. They don't have to carry the bottle of water from home, instead fill it as and when it is required. From the business execution side, FIVE main players would be integral part of it. 1) BOTTLE MANUFACTRERS - ideally two sizes of 1L & 2L bottles need to be made. As mentioned earlier, the bottle has to be in accordion style, where it could be folded flat vertically and this is possible by using Low Density Polyethylene materials. The branding on the bottle also needs to be done smartly in such a way the brand name is visible even when folded. 2) THE APP DEVELOPMENT TEAM, which plays a vital role by creating a technology which is integrated in three things - the bottom of the bottle, the App and in the vending machine which reads both the data and give the final output. This is the central team which needs to liaise with the bottle manufactures and the vending machine. So, they create a code sticker, gives it to the bottle maker to integrate. They also create circular magnetic plate which can read the data and gives it to the vending machine manufactures to fix it in the machine. And also create an App which could be downloaded. This concept is similar to the magnetic strip /chip in the credit card and how the swiping machine reads the data by just taping/ swiping on it 3) ReFILLER PORT - the vending machine has to take up a regular space of a tap and probably look like EV charging port. And interaction of the customer with the machine like the one similar one which they have with an ATM machine perhaps. The machine maker needs to collaborate with the technology team to get the magnetic plate integrate into it and the tap needs to operate based on the data reading. 4) CIVIL ENGINEERING - when deciding on the spot of vending machine, it needs to be noted they would require 10ft x10ft space beneath the ground for storing water. So, the ground needs to be dug out and a underground tank built to store the water. The capacity could be around 10000 liters approximately. This tank would be covered permanently with just an outlet to fill the tank. the vending machine fixed on it. The tank could be filled as and when the water level goes below the mark. And last player, 5) FILTER MAKER - Before the water from the tank comes out through the tap, it needs to go through a filter which makes the water “ drink ready” . It has to be noted, the fresh water is not viable to be consumed directly, hence it needs to be filtered before it goes into the consumer's bottle. Finally, the disposal of the bottle - It is similar to the SIM/credit card. They could always replace the existing bottle with the new one for a nominal price in any of the retail store, this could be little less than buying a new bottle.

The moment the new one is activated the old one becomes obsolete. How the idea help the company to tackle the concerns a consumer is facing?” - g. concerns on convenience, concerns on sustainability, etc The idea solves couple of issues at both individual level and environmental level. Every time the consumer buys a bottle, it creates a burden on the individual as well as the planet. This single bottle concept negates the production of unnecessary plastic bottles. Imagine not having to produce million of plastic bottles. And another thing is the accordion fold bottle, which is a great space saver, easy to carry and of course very convenient. Why this solution is the best option to future proof their business?” This concept makes the consumer subscribe to a plan which is technically a subscription to the brand. Let us imagine, if a person subscribes to 50 liters of water, this means he is guaranteed to buy 50 bottles from the brand in the future. He would in no way be instigated to go for another brand of natural water since he has already subscribed to this plan and by all means, he would stick to the brand as he has already paid for it. And the subscription could be made more attractive, like when the consumer has exhausted say 40L out of 50L, then an attractive offer could be custom made for him via app, to subscribe for more, for example, subscribe for 50L more and get 70L. This is like mobile subscription; the consumer would not so easily move away from the brand. This makes the consumer a customer for life probably. The subscription is applicable irrespective of the cities/place.
